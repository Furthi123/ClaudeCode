## Quick rules
- **Colors:** black / white / neutral grey scale + one accent: `#FFDB00` (Delcube Yellow). Use yellow sparingly — one hero moment per screen.
- **Type:** DM Sans everywhere. No uppercase + wide tracking. Sentence case. DM Mono for editions & SKUs.
- **Radii:** rounded by default, 12px baseline. Hero cards go 32–48px. Never sharp corners.
- **Tone:** direct, confident, short. German `du` / English `you`. No emoji. No exclamation points.
- **Motion:** 180ms ease-out default. Immediate feedback, no theatrics.
- **Icons:** Lucide-style, 1.75px stroke (flagged substitution — confirm with team).
